Getting Started

1. Create a Driver Profile – Add your driver information and kart number in the Drivers tab.

2. Start a Session – Enter a session name, select your driver, and choose weather conditions.

3. Record Laps – Tap START to begin timing, then tap LAP to record each lap time.

4. Review Your Data – Check the Logs tab to view all your sessions and lap times.
