Privacy Policy

KartTimer Privacy Policy

Introduction

KartTimer ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

KartTimer is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Data: Driver profiles, lap times, session data, and weather conditions are stored exclusively on your device.
  • No Personal Identification: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us for support.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your location, device identifiers, or usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

Any information you provide (such as through support emails) is used solely to:

  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Improve our application and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

All your lap timing data, driver profiles, and session information are stored locally on your device. This data:

  • Is never transmitted to our servers or third-party services
  • Remains under your complete control
  • Is protected by your device's security features

Third-Party Services

KartTimer does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not share your data with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

KartTimer does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the date of this policy and posting the new version on our support page.
